ASCEND Clinical Simulation Portal
=================================

REQUIREMENTS:
- Windows 10 or 11
- PHP Installed (https://windows.php.net/download/)

HOW TO RUN:
1. Ensure PHP is installed on your system.
2. Double-click "start_app.bat".
3. The dashboard will open in your default browser automatically.

TROUBLESHOOTING:
- If the black window appears and closes immediately, you likely do not have PHP installed.
- Ensure you are connected to the internet for the AI features to work.
